9 features

Built for real estate

Every feature on this list exists because we've watched it move the conversion needle on real agent sites. Nothing decorative.

SEO-indexable IDX listings

Listings rendered server-side so Google sees them. Each listing gets its own URL, schema markup, and indexable description — that's how individual listings rank, not just your homepage.

Instant home valuation lead magnet

"What's my home worth?" tool prominently placed on homepage and seller page. Three-question form, instant value estimate, gated contact. Seller-lead conversion typically 10–15%.

Saved search + property alerts

Buyers register to save searches and get email/SMS alerts when matching listings hit MLS. Behavior-based gating — let them browse 5 listings free, then prompt for registration.

Neighborhood pages with school data

5 dedicated neighborhood pages with average price, days on market, GreatSchools ratings, walk score, transit info, demographic snapshot, and current listings in that neighborhood.

Interactive map search

Drag-to-search map with school zone overlays, custom polygons, and saved areas. The single feature buyers spend most time using — and the one cheapest agent sites get wrong.

Mortgage calculator + affordability tool

Live monthly payment calculations on every listing page. Adjustable down payment, interest rate, taxes, HOA. Lender partner referral capture if you want it.

Featured listing showcase

Hero carousel for your own active listings, separate from MLS feed. Branded video, 3D tours, drone footage support. The first thing your sellers want to see is their listing front-and-center.

Behavior-triggered lead capture

Smart prompts that fire after a buyer views 5+ listings, saves a property, or returns to the site. Not aggressive pop-ups — contextual asks at the moment buyer intent peaks.

Live reviews from Google, Zillow, Realtor.com

Real, verified reviews pulled from all three platforms onto your homepage and agent page. With star schema so your stars show up in Google search results.

Specializations

A luxury agent sells differently than a first-time-buyer specialist. We tailor the package for your specific market segment and the buyers/sellers you actually want.

Buyer's agent website design

Buyer-focused agents need IDX-heavy sites that don't feel like a portal clone. We design hero sections around neighborhood discovery, mortgage education, and first-time buyer guidance. Behavior-triggered registration gates kick in after 5 listing views. Mortgage calculator embedded everywhere. Buyer guide as a lead magnet.

Listing agent website design

Listing-focused agents win sellers by showing they know the market and their marketing strategy. We design hero sections around instant home valuation, seller resources, and your recent sold portfolio with days-on-market data. Sold listings displayed prominently (where MLS rules allow). Pre-listing checklist as a seller lead magnet.

Luxury real estate website design

Luxury sells on aesthetics, exclusivity, and discretion. We design with bigger imagery, slower scrolling, more whitespace, video-forward listing pages, and private-listing capabilities. Curated featured properties, lifestyle content about each market (e.g., wine country, ski-in/ski-out, waterfront), and concierge contact instead of generic forms.

New construction real estate website

New construction specialists need builder partner showcases, floor plan downloads, lot selection maps, and customization timeline education. We build pages per community and per builder, with school zone overlays and progress photo galleries. Buyer registration tied to community of interest.

Investment property real estate website

Investor-focused agents need cap rate calculators, cash flow estimators, rental comp data, and 1031 exchange resources. We build for the more analytical buyer with downloadable spreadsheets, market-by-market rent analysis, and BRRRR strategy content. Investor-specific newsletter signup.

First-time homebuyer specialist website

First-time buyers need education above all else. We design with extensive buyer guides, mortgage 101 content, down-payment assistance program info per state, and step-by-step homebuying timelines. The site reads like a friendly mentor, not a sales floor.

Relocation specialist real estate website

Relocation agents serve buyers from other cities who can't tour in person. We design with virtual tour-first listings, neighborhood video walkthroughs, school comparison tools, and corporate relocation packages. Time-zone-aware contact forms because relocation buyers email at all hours.

Real estate team / brokerage website

Team sites need to balance individual agent bios with team brand cohesion. We design with agent roster pages, lead routing by neighborhood specialty or listing type, separate bio pages per agent (each with their own schema), and a unified contact form that distributes leads by availability.
